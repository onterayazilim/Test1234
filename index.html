About Olemex

Olemex is a Turkish agricultural trading company focused on the wholesale of pumpkin and sunflower seeds. With over 45 years of experience, we combine deep local knowledge with global export standards.

As the third generation, our mission is to bring the finest Anatolian harvests to international markets with consistency, transparency, and long‑term partnerships.
