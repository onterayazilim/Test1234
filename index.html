About Olemez

Rooted in Türkiye, growing with the world.

For more than four decades, we have combined local field knowledge with disciplined trade practices to serve demanding international buyers.

Olemez is a Turkish trading company specialized in the wholesale of pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds. Our story began 45 years ago in the agricultural markets of Central Anatolia, where we learned to read the soil, the harvest and the expectations of professional buyers.

Over time, we have built a reliable network of farmers, local collectors and processors across the region. We carefully research each harvest, select the most suitable origins and qualities, and purchase directly at the source to secure consistent, traceable and competitive lots.

Today, as the third generation of the family, our vision is to transform this local experience into a strong presence in the global seed market. Our goal is to export from Türkiye to importers, packers and roasters in every part of the world, while maintaining the integrity and authenticity that built our reputation.
