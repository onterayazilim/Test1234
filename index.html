About Olemex

Olemex is a Turkish agricultural trading company focused on the wholesale of pumpkin and sunflower seeds. For over 45 years, our family has been deeply involved in the agricultural markets of Central Anatolia.

As the third generation, our mission is to bring our local expertise to the global market and supply importers, packers, and roasters worldwide with consistent, high‑quality Turkish seeds.
